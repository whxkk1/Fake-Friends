# Fake Friends
- With this mod, you can add anyone to your friends list.
- To add someone, go to their profile and tap the '+' button on the left.
- To remove them, go to their profile and tap the 'x' button on the left.
- You can configure how the mod displays fake friends in the settings.
## Note
- This mod is purely <cr>visual</c>; the other person won't see you on their friends list.
